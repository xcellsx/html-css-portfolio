SisTech Space

Solution to Provide Career Guidance to Women Pursuing Tech Related Jobs

Features

A Mobile App that has Chat Groups, Forum and Daily Inspiration to provide motivation to women pursuing Tech related jobs. A Large Language Model (LLM) using OpenAI to provide resources on Job Preparation such as writing Resumes, creating CVs and preparing for an Interview.

Mobile App - Chat Groups

Provide a space for like-minded users to communicate with one another on specific topics For example, a study group for female students pursuing STEM related jobs after graduation

Mobile App - Forum

Inspiration was taking from Reddit to have a platform for multiple users from diverse backgrounds to come together and discuss on a specific topic

Mobile App - Daily Inspiration

Daily inspiration will be generated on the Home Page of app, to provide motivation to users to show them Women in STEM is possible Users also get to mention their own STEM Role Model, this would help find like-minded individuals to create a community
