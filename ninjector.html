The Ninjector

A device to help boost confidence of caregivers for patients undergoing palliative care.

3D Modelling of Device

Brainstorming of designs were done, by drawing multiple variations of possible designs. Using Fusion360, multiple iterations of the design were created and explored. With further user testing, the optimal shape was achieved.

Electronic Components

Hardware - Electronic circuit created to power the device. Software - Arduino was used to create the code to perform several tasks. This helped with ensuring the device meets the needs of the client and is still purposeful.

Research

Research was done to find the best mechanisms that could be implemented to make the mechanical components work. The spring mechanism was mainly used so that the different components will be locked when needed and released after use.

WebApp

A WebApp was created with Streamlit to help with book-keeping. The WebApp will be used by the medical professionals in charge, monitoring the process of the drug adminstration.

Animations

Blender was used to created product animations that would represent how the device would be used in real world world context. It also marked the start of my interest for creating Blender artworks.
